    Reasons Why We Love Botswana!....

    Botswana Safari Holiday

    There is a number of ways to experience the big 5 and other wildlife that are unique to this landscape. Fly-in safaris, House Boat Safari, Honeymoon safari, Luxury Safari as well as the very intimate Mokoro canoe safari. Botswana is the most intimate African Safari. Exclusivity is a premium at several of the lodges with the primary aim of providing an experience removed from many other busy safari destinations. Champaign breakfasts with a view of a busy waterhole are not uncommon in Botswana.

    Safe And Welcoming

    Botswana is in our opinion the safest place to visit in Southern Africa. With no civil wars in its recent history and the fact that it has never been colonised means its people and their cultures are warm and welcoming which adds to its intimacy. Local tribes and the beautiful people of this country are welcoming and embrace the opportunity to share their culture with the world..

    Scenery And Landscapes

    Botswana offers a diversity in extremes from the vast flood planes that make up the Okavango Delta to the dry deserts of the Kalahari and Makgadikgadi salt pans. The main game reserves are the Chobe, Savuti, Moremi and Okavango Delta which offer a unique experience at each. These landscapes famously transform during the wet months as the Delta fills up and animals begin migrating.

    Wildlife

    The Chobe is famous for its Abundance of Elephants that embark on migratory journeys through what is known as the elephant corridor. These giants are not alone and are also home to Botswana’s unique desert lions who are indigenous to this region. More than just the big 5 the rains and wet season bring an enormous range of birdlife sharing these areas with 100 strong groups of hippos..
